Nusantara Explorer

Nusantara Explorer is here for you, Indonesia's youth! Uncover the cultural treasures from Sabang to Merauke through a captivating interactive map. Delve into history, experience the melodies of traditional music, marvel at the architecture of traditional homes, immerse yourself in legendary folklore, and test your knowledge with interactive quizzes. With Nusantara Explorer, preserve Indonesian heritage in a modern and enjoyable way!
